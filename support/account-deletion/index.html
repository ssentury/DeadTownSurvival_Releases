Dead Town Survival – Account Deletion Request

This page explains how users can request deletion of their account and related data for Dead Town Survival.

How to request account deletion

To request deletion of your account, please send an email to:

03jhyun@gmail.com

Please include the following information:

  • Your in-game nickname
  • The email address used to create the account (if applicable)
  • Subject line: [Dead Town Survival] Account Deletion Request

Data that will be deleted

  • Firebase authentication account (if applicable)
  • Stored player data

Advertising and third-party services

Dead Town Survival may use third-party advertising services such as Unity Ads, ironSource, or other advertising partners. These services may collect certain device information such as the device identifier (Advertising ID), IP address, and interaction data in order to provide advertisements, measure ad performance, and prevent fraudulent activity.

This advertising-related data is collected and processed by the respective advertising providers according to their own privacy policies.

Processing time

Account deletion requests are usually processed within 7 days.

Additional data retention

Certain data such as logs or records required for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance may be retained temporarily even after account deletion.
